About Me

I'm co-founder, creative director and wearer of many hats at Bam Lab, LLC. I've been self employed for 10+ years in the creative field. Formally trained as a graphic designer; the lion's share of my work experience has been an all encompassing process of product creation and development.

I can take an idea all the way to product launch -- including r&d, marketing, merchandising, and all layers of design in between. Ecommerce is my jam.

Beyond industry, you'll likely find me improv gardening, tooling around on diy projects or hiking thru the many awesome trails in New England.
